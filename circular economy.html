What is Circular Economy?

The circular economy is an alternative to the traditional linear economy (take, make, dispose). It aims to redefine growth by focusing on positive society-wide benefits. It involves designing out waste and pollution, keeping products and materials in use, and regenerating natural systems.

Technologies Enabling Circular Economy

AI helps optimize resource management, recycling processes, and supply chain logistics, enabling more efficient use of materials.

IoT devices help track product lifecycle, monitor energy usage, and enhance recycling capabilities by providing real-time data.

Blockchain ensures transparency in material flows, improving the traceability of products from production to recycling.
